About Me

My academic voyage began with a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from Oriental College of Technology, Bhopal, where I notched a CPI of 8.65/10. My thirst for deeper knowledge and specialization in computer science propelled me to pursue an M.Tech at NIT Trichy in Computer Science, securing an impressive CGPA of 9.45 in 2019-20. The journey didn't stop there; I further escalated my qualifications by completing another M.Tech in Computer Science from IIT Bombay, achieving a CPI of 9.04/10. This path wasn't just about collecting degrees; it was about immersing myself in the depths of computer science, from Foundations of Machine Learning to Natural Language Processing and beyond.

The transition from Oriental College to NIT Trichy, and eventually to IIT Bombay, was a period of intense learning and application. Between my academic pursuits, I was deeply involved in exploring the practical implications of my studies. My tenure at NIT Trichy was particularly significant as it allowed me to hone my skills in Program Analysis, Implementation Techniques for RDBMS, and Software Lab, setting a solid foundation for my research and professional work that followed.

Starting as a Data Scientist Intern at HiLabs Inc., Pune, in January 2022, I quickly transitioned to a full-time Data Scientist role by July 2022. This period marked my direct engagement with the healthcare sector, where I applied machine learning models to healthcare-based projects, showcasing my ability to translate academic knowledge into impactful industry solutions. My responsibilities ranged from data preprocessing and cleaning to working on Spark using both Scala and Python on Hadoop-based clusters, a testament to the practical application of my comprehensive academic training.

My academic projects and publications reflect a deep engagement with cutting-edge research. From financial sentiment analysis to crop yield prediction using GIS, each project was a step towards solving complex real-world problems. My publication at the ICPC '22 and my master's thesis on algorithm identification using NLP models stand out as milestones in my academic career, highlighting my commitment to contributing to the scientific community.

My journey through different educational institutions and my professional career has equipped me with a robust set of skills. From Database Management and Data Preprocessing to advanced competencies in Explainable AI and Natural Language Processing, my portfolio is a testament to my comprehensive skill set. Coupled with hands-on experience in Database Management, Data Mining, and the practical application of statistical techniques and ML models, I stand ready to tackle complex challenges in the field of computer science.

Alongside my formal education and professional experience, I've pursued certifications in Data Science, Competitive Programming, and various programming languages and tools from reputable platforms like Coding Blocks, Hacker Rank, and Udemy. These certifications are not just badges of honor but reflections of my continuous effort to stay updated with the latest in technology and methodologies.

With a solid foundation in both theory and application, my journey from Oriental College through NIT Trichy to IIT Bombay has been nothing short of exhilarating. As I look forward, I am excited about the opportunities to further explore and contribute to the fields of Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI, pushing the boundaries of what's possible and making a tangible impact on the world.
